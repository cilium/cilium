Further Reading
###############

Other resources about eBPF and Cilium include:

* The `"Further Reading" section of the ebpf.io website
  <https://ebpf.io/what-is-ebpf/#further-reading>`__, which includes a curated
  list of resources about eBPF and its applications.

* The list of `Cilium case studies on cilium.io
  <https://cilium.io/adopters/>`__, which includes case studies from a variety
  of companies and organizations that have adopted Cilium. This page presents
  many use cases for Cilium in production environments.

* The `blog posts referenced on cilium.io <https://cilium.io/blog/>`__, which
  include a variety of posts about Cilium, eBPF, and related topics. In
  particular, the "Technology" section exposes specific features of the
  project, whereas the "Community" section regroups contributions from various
  members of the community, whether or not they are affiliated with the
  project.
